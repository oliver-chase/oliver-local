# CMO Brand Guide

Canonical orchestration reference for CMO persona brand direction.

## Positioning Anchor

V.Two is positioned as a senior software consulting partner focused on data, AI, and product delivery for high-leverage teams.

## Voice

- Clear over clever
- Specific over generic
- Confident without hype

## Non-Negotiables

- No vague claims without proof points
- No generic agency language
- No public messaging without approval
